About Us

To provide the best experience for our customers, we uphold these core principles:

High quality ingredients

We believe our customers deserve nothing but the best. We prioritize quality over quantity by always using the finest ingredients available to make our products truly exceptional.

Consistency

We believe customers should know what to expect every time they order. Our baked goods always deliver the same delightful taste, texture, and appearance.

Proper safety processes

We prioritize the health and safety of our community by clearly labeling all our treats with ingredients and allergen information. Additionally, our kitchen and all surfaces, utensils, and equipment are regularly cleaned and sanitized to prevent contamination.

Communication

At Mama K’s we communicate with our customers with the same warmth, honesty, and care as we would with family. We keep open and clear communication with our customers and respond promptly to inquiries and feedback in a professional manner.

Meet Mama K

Hey there, friends! I am Kaylee Conley, but some know me as Mama K. I am a proud mother, wife, and entrepreneur with an undeniable passion for baking. Over the years, through trial and error, practice, and determination, I created a way to share the joy baking brings me with others. My family is my biggest motivation. My sons, Noah and Maverick, and my husband, Justin, are my biggest fans. I love creating new delicacies for them to enjoy. Noah loves helping with the sweetest recipes, Maverick adores tasting our concoctions and my husband looks forward to his favorite treats after a long day at work. Seeing the joy and comfort our delicious goodies bring them fills my heart, and I love watching their love for baking grow. When I started Mama K’s Bakery, my goal was to bring people together to enjoy the simple pleasures of life. Acts of service is one of my love languages, and my absolute favorite thing to do is bake tasty treats for those around me for any occasion-- whether it’s a cake for a special day, a box of assorted treats on a holiday, or a batch of cookies to lift spirits during tough times. Spreading love and joy is at the core of who I am, and baking is the perfect way to share those wholesome feelings with others. The joy that one gets when taking a bite of a homemade, delectable, love-filled goodie is one that all should experience often. Love and family are at the forefront of Mama K’s Bakery. We strive to bring warmth to every home and create a sense of community. Thank you for being a part of our dream.
